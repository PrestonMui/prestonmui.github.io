abstract: 'As policymakers grapple with the challenge of achieving a soft landing, one underrated tool is reducing healthcare inflation. With healthcare costs projected to rise over the coming decade, a policy proposal that has been circulating is making Medicare reimbursements site-neutral. In this brief, we estimate the disinflation effects of implementing site-neutral payments for Medicare.'
